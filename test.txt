   2.2 Distributed representations
   2.3 Parallel distributed processing
   2.4 Deep learning paradigm
   2.5 Neuromorphic computing (spiking neural networks)
   2.6 Energy-based neural models (Hopfield networks, Boltzmann machines)

   5.1 Markov decision processes (MDPs)

   5.4 Game-theoretic AI (normal-form, extensive-form, stochastic games)
   5.5 Decision-theoretic planning
   5.6 Multi-criteria and risk-sensitive decision making

   6.1 Genetic algorithms (GAs)
   6.2 Genetic programming (GP)

   6.5 Swarm intelligence (ant colony optimization, particle swarm optimization, bee algorithms)
   6.6 Artificial immune systems
   6.7 Memetic algorithms

   7.1 Classical control (PID, LQG)
   7.2 Optimal control and dynamic programming
   7.3 Adaptive control

8. Embodied, Situated, and Enactive AI Paradigms
   8.1 Embodied cognition
   8.2 Situated agents
   8.3 Enactive AI
   8.4 Behavior-based robotics (subsumption architecture)

    12.1 Foundation models (FMs) and large language models (LLMs)[9][10]
